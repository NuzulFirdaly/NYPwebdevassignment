Monster Battle Assignment

This was a project that i was working on during my course in ITE, and were able to bring it to a business exhibition in New Delhi,India which it then won GOLD under the safety and health section. To summarize, it is an application that helps users ranging from patients (mass public) to doctors, laboratory scientist, clinics in general, by shortening the time taken between test and getting results fast. This idea was proposed years before Covid-19, if i had continued working on it, who would knew whether it would benefit our frontline workers currently.
